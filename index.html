About FreshTrack

Freshness-Driven Food Waste Prevention Platform

We transform freshness signals into operational decisions — helping UK grocery retailers prevent waste before it happens.

What We Do

FreshTrack combines Remaining Shelf Life (RSL) modelling, root-cause attribution, and execution orchestration into one closed-loop performance system.

Instead of reporting waste after it happens, we detect risk early, identify why it occurs, and trigger accountable interventions across stores, distribution centres, and suppliers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Freshness-Driven Waste Prevention Platform.

What problem does FreshTrack solve? +
FreshTrack prevents avoidable fresh waste in UK grocery by turning freshness data, operational signals, and root-cause analytics into real execution actions — reducing write-offs while protecting availability.
How is this different from traditional waste dashboards? +
Traditional dashboards report what already happened. FreshTrack identifies why waste happens, who needs to act, and triggers interventions like FEFO lists, allocation changes, and markdown timing automatically.
Does it replace existing ERP or WMS systems? +
No. FreshTrack integrates with ERP, WMS, markdown engines, and store systems. It acts as an operational intelligence layer that enhances existing workflows without replacing core infrastructure.
How quickly can we see measurable impact? +
In pilot deployments (1 DC + 20–50 stores), measurable write-off reduction can typically be observed within 10–14 weeks, depending on category and execution adoption.
Is the platform compliant with UK GDPR and retail security standards? +
Yes. The platform is designed with tenant isolation, encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access control, and audit logs aligned with UK GDPR and enterprise retail requirements.
Can it support ESG and CO₂e reporting? +
Absolutely. FreshTrack links operational interventions to measurable waste reduction and estimated CO₂e impact, supporting board-level ESG reporting and sustainability narratives.
